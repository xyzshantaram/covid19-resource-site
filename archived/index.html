  1. Select the resources you want to view.
  2. Select the state you want to view leads in, and click "Get help" to view leads.
  3. Verified resources have a green badge at the bottom, and have been verified by our volunteers.
  4. Unverified resources have not been verified yet, but still have a chance of working.
